**Why does this matter?**
- ğŸ¯ **Precision:** Get exactly what you need, not what you think you asked for
- ğŸ”„ **Consistency:** Reliable outputs every time
- âš¡ **Efficiency:** Fewer iterations, better results
- ğŸš€ **Power:** Unlock capabilities you didn't know existed

### ğŸ§­ The Psychology of AI Communication

**Key Insight:** The better you communicate with AI, the better it communicates back to you.

### Step 1: Try a Basic Prompt

Open ChatGPT and ask:
```text

```

**What you'll notice:**
- Generic response
- No specific focus
- Unclear depth or audience

### Step 2: Engineer the Same Request

Now try this:
```text

2. How it affects their savings
3. One practical tip to protect their money

Keep it under 200 words and use simple language.
```

**What you'll notice:**
- Targeted response
- Clear structure
- Practical focus
- Appropriate tone
