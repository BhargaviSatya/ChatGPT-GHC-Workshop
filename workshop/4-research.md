## üîç Exercise 4: Follow-Up Research Prompts

### Deep Dive Prompts

#### 1. **Understanding Breakthroughs**

```text
Explain one of these breakthroughs in simple language:

[List of breakthroughs]

Use analogies and everyday examples to help me understand:
- What was the problem before?
- How does this breakthrough solve it?
- What are the real-world implications?
```

#### 2. **Historical Timeline**

```text
Show me a timeline of key events in [technology field].

Include:
- Major milestones (with years)
- Key breakthroughs
- Industry shifts
- Significant failures/lessons learned

Format as a chronological timeline with brief explanations.
```

#### 3. **Impact Analysis**

```text
How could [technology A] impact [field B]?

For example:
- How could quantum computing impact AI and cybersecurity?
- How could CRISPR affect healthcare delivery?
- How could 6G networks change IoT?

Provide:
1. Direct impacts
2. Indirect consequences
3. Potential risks or challenges
4. Timeline for impact
```

#### 4. **Comparative Analysis**

```text
Compare [Technology A] vs [Technology B] in terms of:
- Current maturity level
- Technical advantages/disadvantages
- Market adoption rate
- Future potential
- Investment and funding

Which one do you think will be more influential in 10 years?
```

#### 5. **Future Predictions**

```text
Based on current developments in [technology], predict:

1. Where will this technology be in 5 years?
2. What obstacles must be overcome?
3. Which applications will emerge first?
4. What skills will be needed for this field?
5. What ethical concerns might arise?

Be specific and ground predictions in current reality.
```

## üéØ Exercise 6: Critical Research Skills

### Evaluating Information Quality

```text
Help me evaluate the quality and reliability of this information about [technology]:

[Paste information or claim]

Analyze:
1. Credibility of the source
2. Supporting evidence
3. Potential biases
4. Context and timeframe
5. Contradictory information elsewhere
6. How to verify this information

Provide a "trustworthiness score" and explain why.
```

### Identifying Hype vs Reality

```text
Help me distinguish between hype and reality for [technology]:

Analyze current claims about this technology:
1. What's actually working today?
2. What's still in early research?
3. What's pure speculation?
4. What are realistic timelines?
5. What are genuine limitations?

Be honest about where the technology actually is vs where people say it is.
```
